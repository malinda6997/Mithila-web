welcome to our shop

Since 1984. we believe that healthy food is tasty

The first Burger Shop in Kany opened its doors in 2014. This decision was made after a series of suppers, pop-ups, and events by A Rule of Tum. The restaurant was an overnight success with queues out the door from day one. Our focus was on providing excellent food and utilising local suppliers. Alongside a choice of craft beers and great cocktails. The restaurant went from strength to strength and kick-started a food revolution in Hereford.
